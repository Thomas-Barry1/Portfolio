About Me

As a DevOps Engineer with a strong focus on automation and infrastructure as code (IAC), I have successfully deployed solutions that enhance security, efficiency, and scalability. I take pride in designing and implementing systems that streamline workflows while improving product performance. For instance, I recently led the deployment of an antivirus scanning solution with a 100% scan success rate, iteratively improving the process to reduce scan time by 90x. These improvements ensured faster, more secure operations while meeting stringent security protocols.

I excel at tackling complex cloud-architected environments, modernizing them with best practices in DevOps. One of my recent projects involved transforming a poorly coded website by transitioning it to Terraform, modularizing the code, and enhancing the user experience. The result was not only a vastly improved UI/UX but also highly positive feedback from users. My expertise in AWS services, combined with tools like Docker and Python, allows me to deliver high-performance solutions that scale efficiently.

In addition to my professional experience, I actively explore personal projects that drive my passion for full-stack development and AI. I built an AI-driven application designed to assist teachers with tailored lesson planning, using cutting-edge technologies like Angular and NodeJS. This hands-on experience complements my DevOps expertise and reflects my commitment to continuous learning and innovation in the field.
